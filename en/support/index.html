Frequently asked questions

Why is no live text showing?

On the very first launch, iOS downloads the speech model for the selected language — this needs an internet connection and may take a moment. Afterwards the app works fully offline.

Which devices are supported?

Mama Counter requires iOS 26 or newer and an iPhone 12 or newer.

Are my conversations recorded?

No. Speech recognition runs entirely on your iPhone; audio is neither stored nor transmitted. Details in the privacy policy.
